Zhicheng Wang (王志成)

I am a Researcher at MEGVII Inc. My research interests focus on the computer vision and artificical intelligence, specifically on the topic of keypoint detection, object detection, segmentation, person re-identification. I obtained Master Degree from Thsinghua University in 2017, supervised by Prof. Chun Yuan. I am now a researcher in Megvii (Face++) under the supervision of Erjin Zhou and Dr. Sun Jian, and used to be supervised by Gang Yu.
